Hoppers

Delicious bowl-shaped pancakes made from fermented rice batter and coconut milk.

Prep Time and Servings

Prep Time: 8 hours

Serves: 4 people

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Mix the rice flour, coconut milk, and yeast in a bowl.
  2. Let the mixture ferment for 6-8 hours.
  3. Heat the hopper pan on medium heat.
  4. Pour a ladleful of the mixture into the pan and swirl it to coat the edges.
  5. Cover and cook until the edges are crispy and the center is soft.
