Rice & Curry

A traditional Sri Lankan dish served with a variety of curries. Simple yet packed with flavor.

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 15 minutes, then cook it according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat some oil and sauté the onions and garlic until golden.
  3. Add the curry powder and fry for another minute.
  4. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Add the tomatoes and cook for a few minutes until softened.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk, reduce the heat, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the curry over hot rice with your choice of sides.

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Serves: 4

Fish Ambul Thiyal

A sour fish curry made with black pepper, goraka, and spices, a beloved Sri Lankan classic.

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Cut the fish into large cubes and rinse with water.
  2. Mix the black pepper, turmeric, garlic, and salt into a paste.
  3. Rub the paste onto the fish and let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. Add the fish and goraka to a pan with water and simmer on low heat for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Serve with rice or other side dishes.

Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Serves: 4

Kottu Roti

A popular Sri Lankan street food, made with chopped roti, vegetables, and optional meats or eggs.

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan and sauté the onions until golden.
  2. Add the eggs and scramble until cooked.
  3. Add the chopped roti, carrot, and chicken (if using) and stir fry for a few minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot with spicy curry or sauce.

Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 4
