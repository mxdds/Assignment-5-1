Watalappan

A creamy coconut custard flavored with jaggery and spices, a traditional Sri Lankan dessert.

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Grate or chop the jaggery finely and melt it using a little water to form a thick syrup. Let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs until frothy.
  3. Add the coconut milk, melted jaggery syrup, cardamom powder, and nutmeg to the eggs. Mix well.
  4. Strain the mixture into a steaming dish to remove any lumps.
  5. Sprinkle the chopped cashews on top (if using).
  6. Steam the mixture on low heat for 30-40 minutes or until set.
  7. Let it cool and serve chilled.

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Serves: 6
