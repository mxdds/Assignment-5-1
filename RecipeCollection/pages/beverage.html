Ceylon Tea

Discover the traditional way to brew the perfect cup of Ceylon tea.

Ingredients

Directions

  1. Bring fresh water to a rolling boil.
  2. Place 1 teaspoon of loose leaf Ceylon tea in a teapot or cup.
  3. Pour the boiling water over the tea leaves.
  4. Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on desired strength.
  5. Strain the tea leaves or remove the tea bag .
  6. Add sugar or honey to taste, if desired.
  7. For a richer flavor, add milk or a slice of lemon.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your traditional Ceylon tea!

Prep Time: 5 minutes | Brew Time: 5 minutes | Serves: 1
